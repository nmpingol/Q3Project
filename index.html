Prevention is always better than solutions. In this article, you wil find different ways to prevent fire at home. These are simple, easy things that anyone can do. Even simple things we don't suspect could be the cause of a fire. Follow these simple steps and keep your family, neighbors, and yourself safe!


Ways to Prevent Fire

Unplug Unused Items		 Keeping these items plugged in even when not in use can always be a fire hazard. They can cause a surge or overheat. We need to unplug these especially considering that we aren't always alert. It would even save electricity, which is a plus in saving the environment

Never Leave Flames Unattended		 It is important to pay attention when you are doing an activity that involves flames. For example, cooking, baking, using a frieplace, or lighting candles. Don't be proud or cocky, keep an eye on these things even if you are experienced. If this is done, you will also be able to solve a problem quickly if ever something happens.

Keep Flamable Items Away From Heat		 Flammable items include rubbing alcohol, nail polish, nail polish remover, etc. There are also many things that can easily catch fire, like paper, cotton, and hair. It is important to keep these things away from any heat source.

Don't Smoke In the Home		 Not only the home, don't smoke in enclosed areas. These aren't just a fire hazard, but also a health hazard. Make sure to put out the ciggarette before disposing of it, and double check this from time to time.

Clean Up		 Excessive clutter can potentially be a risk for an unexpected fire. Clutter also increases the risk of a fire spreading quickly. You should make sure to organize here and there just to get lower the risk.
